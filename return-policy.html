Return Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1. Digital Services

AnyPay provides digital services for mobile recharges and bill payments. Due to the instant nature of these services:

  • Mobile recharges are processed immediately and cannot be reversed once completed
  • Bill payments are processed instantly and sent to the respective service providers
  • Transaction fees, if any, are non-refundable

2. Failed Transactions

In case of failed transactions:

  • If your payment was processed but the service was not delivered, we will investigate and resolve within 24-48 hours
  • Refunds for failed transactions will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Any bank charges or processing fees will be refunded if the failure was on our end

3. Wrong Number/Account Recharges

For recharges or payments made to incorrect numbers/accounts:

  • We cannot reverse recharges sent to wrong mobile numbers
  • Users are responsible for verifying the recipient number/account before confirming transactions
  • We will assist in contacting the service provider, but cannot guarantee recovery of funds

4. Duplicate Transactions

In case of duplicate transactions:

  • Report duplicate charges immediately through our customer support
  • Provide transaction IDs and relevant screenshots
  • Refunds for verified duplicate transactions will be processed within 7 business days

5. Refund Process

When a refund is approved:

  • Amount will be returned to the original payment method
  • Processing time depends on your bank/payment provider
  • You will receive email notifications about the refund status
